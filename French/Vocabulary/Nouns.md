#### Common Nouns

- **l'homme** (man)
- **la femme** (woman)
- **l'enfant** (child)
- **le garçon** (boy)
- **la fille** (girl)
- **la maison** (house)
- **l'école** (school)
- **le livre** (book)
- **la voiture** (car)
- **le chien** (dog)
- **le chat** (cat)
- **le temps** (time, weather)
- **la chose** (thing)
- **le jour** (day)
- **la nuit** (night)
